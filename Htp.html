How to play:

Player's Turn:

The player can click the "Hit" button to draw a random card and add its value to their score.
If the player's score exceeds 21, the game automatically moves to the dealer's turn.

Dealer's Turn:

The dealer reveals their hidden card and draws cards until their score reaches 17 or more.
The dealer's score is displayed.

Determine Winner:

After both the player and dealer complete their turns, the winner is determined.
If the player's score exceeds 21 or is less than the dealer's score, the dealer wins
